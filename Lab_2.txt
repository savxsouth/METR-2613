' Data Logger: CR310  
' Station: NWC0 (METR 2613 Class) 
' Description: This weather station will have a suite of instruments on 
'   it and collect 5-minute data. 
' 
' Wiring Diagram 
' 
' 12V Battery 
'   Red - BAT + 
'   Black - BAT - 
 
'Declare Public Variables 
Public BATV 
Units BATV=Volts 
 
'Define Data Tables. 
DataTable (NWC0_05A,1,-1)  
  DataInterval (0,5,Min,0) 
  Sample (1,BATV,FP2) 
  FieldNames("BATV") 
EndTable 
 
'Main Program 
BeginProg 
 Scan (5,Sec,0,0) 
  ' Battery Voltage  
  Battery (BATV) 
  CallTable NWC0_05A 
 NextScan 
EndProg
